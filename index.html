Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel or change my booking?

+

You can cancel or change your booking by logging into your account and accessing "My Bookings". Changes are subject to airline policies and may incur fees. For assistance, contact our support team.

What documents do I need for international travel?

+

For international travel, you'll need a valid passport and may require a visa depending on your destination. Check visa requirements at least 6 weeks before travel. Some countries also require proof of vaccination.

How early should I arrive at the airport?

+

We recommend arriving 2 hours early for domestic flights and 3 hours early for international flights. During peak travel times or at busy airports, consider arriving even earlier.

What is your baggage policy?

+

Baggage policies vary by airline and ticket type. Most economy tickets include one carry-on bag and one personal item. Checked baggage fees and weight limits depend on your specific airline and route.

Can I select my seat in advance?

+

Yes, seat selection is available during booking or afterward through "Manage Booking". Some seats may have additional fees. Premium seats and exit rows typically require extra payment.
